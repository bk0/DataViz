About

  • Hi. So...how did I end up in a data visualization class? Let's start from the beginning. I was born and raised in Los Angeles. I began my career working at the University of Southern California (I went to UCLA, but, based on the license plate frames in the work parking lot, there were lots of traitors.) working in their media lab on a virtual reality enviroment for an US Army simulator. This position put my Cognitive Science degree to good use. Then, I had a two year stint at a software company doing database work. I finally fell into a 10 year career in consulting doing forensic accounting and got my CPA license. Surprisingly, I did a lot of data analysis on huge data sets in this position. After doing mainly descriptive statistics and encountering other professionals with backgrounds in statistics, I realized I was lacking to more technical data analytics and statistics skills. This led me back to school to earn my Master's in Analytics. And here I am in San Francisco.
